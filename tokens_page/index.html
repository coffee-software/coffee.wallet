Coffee Token

Coffee Token (CFT) is a small utility and governance token for Coffee Wallet Project. At the moment you can buy and hold CFT to support further development of this open source project. In the future, with CFT you will be able to:

  • Participate in project by voting on features.
  • Pay for an ad inside of the app.
  • CFT holders will get discounts for wallet fees and premium features.

CFT will also be used to provide a tiny reward for new users for completing a gamified tutorial giving them some real value and ensuring they understand basic concepts when entering world of cryptocurrencies.

Current Stage: IEO

Token is now available on Uniswap.

Technical Details

CFT is deployed on Ethereum blockchain as an ERC20 token.
