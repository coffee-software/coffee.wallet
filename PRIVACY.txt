Privacy Policy

Coffee Wallet was build by Coffee Software as an Open Source app. 
App is provided as is with no warranties.


Information Collection And Use

Coffee Wallet stores data in secure device memory. 
Signed transaction are sent to third-party providers and are publicly available in  blockchains. 
Logs with activity are stored on device.
Third-party providers are also queried for various blockchain data and this request might be logged.
No data is send to any Coffee Wallets servers.


Camera Data

Coffee Wallet uses device camera on user demand to read QR codes. 
Aquired codes are used in transactions. 
No other data from camera is stored.
